What is your jewelry made of?

Our jewelry is made of 14k gold filled, 14k gold vermeil, and sterling silver - they're high quality and can last a lifetime with care! All of the materials will be listed under the item description. Read more about the different types of gold jewelry to see why we use gold filled & vermeil.

Can I shower with my jewelry?

For the most part - yes! Gold filled and sterling silver jewelry can withstand normal wear and tear from showering and sleeping in it. There are a few exceptions to this, however. Our Opal Necklaces should NOT be in contact with water, or it will lose its flash. Anything that is gold plated should not be in contact with water. This includes all of our Gemstone Necklaces.

How do I take care of my jewelry

You can follow these instructions to increase your jewelry's longevity & luster: - Wipe your jewelry after each wear with a soft, gentle cloth. - Store your jewelry in a sealed zip bag when not in use. - Avoid harsh chemicals, such as chlorine in pools & hot tubs, lotion, perfume, and hairspray. - Avoid sleeping in jewelry to prevent unnecessary wear and tear. - If your jewelry is looking dull, you can polish it right up with a polishing cloth! - To clean them, wash with lukewarm water and unscented soap. For detailed instructions, including tips on what to avoid, please see our blog post How to Care for Your Jewelry.

How can I keep my jewelry from tangling?

We have the perfect solution - our layered necklace detangler! We wrote an article about how to keep your necklaces from tangling using our detangler. All you have to do is clasp your necklaces onto the detangler clasp, and you can open/close the clasp around your neck! The plus side about this is, you are putting on 2-4 necklaces at once, rather than one at a time.

Here are a few tips you can use besides the detangler:

  • Wear different types of chains to prevent tangling (e.g. combine satellite chain with cable chain).
  • Wear longer/heavier necklaces with shorter/lighter ones.
  • Unclasp the necklace and hold it from one end to let all the "twists" out, and then re-clasp onto your neck.
How do I pick my necklace size or ring size?

Check out our Jewelry Sizing Guide. There is a useful necklace chart in there, as well as a ring chart. We also offer a Free Ring Sizer that's reusable - all you have to do is pay for shipping!

  2. Paypal
    Simply click the PayPal option during checkout where you will be asked to log into your PayPal account. Once the order is completed, you will be redirected to the Mejuri site.

  3. Mejuri Digital and Physical Cards
    You can choose to pay for all or part of your online order with Mejuri gift cards. For a Physical Gift Card, simply enter the code shown on the back of the card by clicking “Apply a gift card” during step 3 of checkout. For a Digital Gift Card, you can enter the code by clicking “Have a coupon code?” button in cart.

    The value of your gift card will be automatically deducted from the order. Should a balance be owed, you can use a credit card or PayPal. Kindly note that you can only use (1) one gift card per transaction.

    You can check your balance in your Mejuri profile. If you haven’t created one yet, go ahead and create one now. Sign in and select “Gift Card” in your profile then enter the card number. Your remaining balance will be shown there. For further details and fine print, click here!

    To use store credit, simply sign in using your Mejuri profile before placing the order and the amount will automatically deduct from the total. Mejuri store credit does not expire and can be used at any time!

    In case you have store credits issued in more than one currency, separate orders must be placed in each corresponding currency. To change the view setting, simply toggle the flag button located on the bottom left corner of the page.
    Store credits are issued in the same currency as the original purchase and we are unable to combine them into one currency.

How is refund processed?

For questions on returns and exchanges, please check here.

  4. Store Credit
    For orders purchased using store credit and later returned, the amount will be reissued as store credit to your account in the same currency. Mejuri is not able to issue store credit in a currency that is different from the original purchase.

Bundle Sets

Should the returned item not meet the above return guidelines, Mejuri has the right to deny a return and have the item re-shipped to customers.

Note: Items purchased through a third-party vendor (outside of Mejuri.com and/or Mejuri stores) must be returned through the company that it was purchased from.
